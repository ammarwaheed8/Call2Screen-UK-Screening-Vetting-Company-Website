About Us!

Your premier source for cutting-edge screening solutions, ensuring trust and security in every endeavor.

Who are we?

Our business was founded in 2018 by Martin Price and Mark Aldridge, who spotted a gap in the market to move the screening industry forward and provide a solution that couples excellent customer service with reliable, automated processes.

Both Martin and Mark have a background in law enforcement, with over 32 years of experience between them. The two joined forces to create Secure Screening Services with the belief that hiring the right people is the cornerstone of any organization’s success. Our success is not only due to the quality of our screening, but to our attitude, approach and the way we treat our clients.

Our Mission

Thousands of UK companies rely on us to streamline their talent acquisition effortlessly and expeditiously, backed by state-of-the-art technology and an approachable team. Our call2screen services expedite the hiring process by presenting clients with pre-vetted candidates, freeing them from administrative hassles.

Our Vision

Call2screen Services envisions a future where safety and trust are paramount in every interaction. Our vision is to provide cutting-edge screening solutions that empower organizations and individuals to make informed decisions with confidence. We aspire to create a world where background checks and verifications are not just routine procedures, but powerful tools for ensuring security, integrity, and peace of mind. Through innovation, advanced technology, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to revolutionize the screening industry and more reliable than ever before.

Our vision is a world where everyone can rely on Secure Screening Services to build a foundation of trust and security in their personal and professional lives, fostering a safer and more secure global community.
